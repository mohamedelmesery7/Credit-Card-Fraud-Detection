2. Business Impact Assessment

The deployment decision hinges on a critical trade-off: the cost of False Negatives (missed fraud) versus
the cost of False Positives (false alarms/customer inconvenience).

So which choose :Logic says Fraud causes losses larger than Actions Taken due to false Alarms

Priority Metric: Given the high cost of financial loss, Recall is the most critical metric for the deployment decision.

And by seeing COnfution Matrix to Them in (Credit fraud.ipynb)  So I think 
The Random Forest with SMOTE model is the recommended choice for production deployment.
